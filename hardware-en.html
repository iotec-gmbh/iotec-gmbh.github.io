Hardware

Many problems require an electronic solution adapted to their particular requirements. While in one application, the longest possible battery life is desired, another application might need maximum processing power. In order to offer a solution optimized for the task at hand, it is in most cases necessary to be able to use custom made electronics. Since electronics development has always been one of iotec's core competencies, we are in a position to offer expert knowledge to the client. We can draw upon many years of experience in the development of analog, digital and power electronics. Often the systems contain programmable devices (microprocessor, FPGA) to solve complex tasks and ensure high flexibility. These embedded systems are either programmed directly (bare metal) or equipped with an operating system (Linux / Windows). In addition to development services, iotec also offers the possibility of manufacturing and testing these electronics in series production.
