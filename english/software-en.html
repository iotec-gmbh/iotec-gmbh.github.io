Software

In order to offer you an individual solution, we develop a tailor-made software solution for you, which can be combined with an Hardware adapted to your needs. We have many years of experience in the field of software and firmware development. Thus, a program tailored to the application is written for the developed electronic solution. This also includes the creation of a customized operating system (Linux / Windows) and the possibility to extend the application with intelligent algorithms. Besides the evaluation of sensor data, the control of the application and visualization of the results using App or Web-Portal is another competence of iotec. To store the data, relational and non-relational databases are used, which can be combined with algorithms for evaluation. We support you from development to production (software distribution) and also offer hosting and maintenance of individual solutions.
