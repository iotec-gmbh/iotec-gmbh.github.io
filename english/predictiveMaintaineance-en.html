As part of the Industrial Internet of Things Predictive maintenance is the proactive maintenance of systems. Before unexpected machine breakdowns occur, these can be intercepted in advance by the acquisition and processing of sensors and machine data.


We offer you the possibility to supplement your systems with sensors to read out and interpret existing sensor and process data, so that a failure of the machines can be detected early.
