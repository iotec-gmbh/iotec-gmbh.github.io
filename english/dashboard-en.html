Dashboard

cross-platform  •  web visualization  •  responsive


A dashboard serves the visual representation of a broad mass of collected data. This means that continuously recorded measured values of a machine, a vehicle or individual components can not only be viewed but also evaluated in order to be able to react to them in real time. Dashboard and predictive maintenance can thus be ideally combined.


A dashboard tailored to your application area offers you a stable framework for analyzing your measured values. We implement all your individual requirements on the basis of modern technologies and frameworks. Possible interfaces to your company's internal content management systems or other channels can be included.
