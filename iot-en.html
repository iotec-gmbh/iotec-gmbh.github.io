Internet of Things

Networking  •  Information technology  •  Cloud Computing


The Internet of Things is the expanding network between man and machine. Technologies are used to build the infrastructure of the information channels. Contents of the collective term are Machine Learning and Predictive Maintenance.


We offer you a modular IoT system (sensors/gateways/portal). It can be equipped with various sensors, e.g. for the acquisition of environmental parameters or the state of machine parts. Based on the selected sensors, their positions and data rates, a system architecture is created. Depending on the application, the data of this system can be forwarded to the server/cloud either by cable (Ethernet, USB, RS485) or wirelessly (WLAN, LTE-M, NB-IOT, GSM). The visualization can be implemented as a portal solution or app. The representation of the sensor values or the derived functionalities can be displayed according to your requirements.
