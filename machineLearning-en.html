Sensors in IoT-applications can generate huge amounts of data that even for specialists are difficult to analyze. Machine learning can recognize patterns in these data sets and learn from past experiences. This allows complex decisions to be made by machines in real time. Furthermore, expert knowledge can be retained and standardized independently of the individual person.


The fields of application for machine learning are very flexible and range from quality assurance to automation technology and decision support for the user. iotec does not limit itself to the well-known image processing applications, but uses these methods in various types of sensor data in order to generate the greatest benefit for you leveraging the deep information density contained in your sensor readings.
