Production

Besides the development of individual hardware and software solutions iotec offers you the option to manufacture electronics and devices in series. In our production rooms we can provide the production of prototypes (single pieces) up to batch production for you. During production, the components are tested and the functionality of the device is documented in a test report. In addition to manufacturing, we offer the service of testing device series for CE conformity. This test can form the basis of your CE declaration of conformity.
