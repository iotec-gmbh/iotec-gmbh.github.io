Collecting and processing data from sensors and end devices embedded in machines or processes is an essential part of the Industrial Internet of Things. The data is evaluated into useful information for various applications. Appropriate applications support the user in decision-making processes. Industrial Internet of Things not only means the optimization of existing processes, but also the design of completely new business models.


iotec designs for you sensor systems that are based on your requirements and connected to your corresponding end devices. The information from the various sensors is collected and can be pre-processed. By using such systems, optimizations can be automated in real time.
