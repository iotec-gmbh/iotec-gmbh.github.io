About us

iotec GmbH is an established service provider for development and manufacturing of sensor technology. Our team consists of highly qualified, motivated and experienced employees. All 15 employees of iotec have completed a scientific education (Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, Physics, Mathematics, Biology) and we maintin close connections to research groups within the University of Applied Sciences Osnabrück. We develop individual solutions within the fields of electronics and automation technology. We guide our customers from the intial idea to the serial product. Our focal points include:

Industrial Internet of Things   •   Internet of Things   •   Dashboard Development   •   Machine Learning   •   Predicive Maintenance   •   Indoor Positioning Systems.

The company has received the Osnabrücker Wirtschaftspreis 2016 which was offered to oustanding young companies from the district of Osnabrück, that were founded in 2011 and have created innovative business models.
