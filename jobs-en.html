working student

Are you in the middle of your studies and would like to gain work experience in the form of a mini-job? At iotec you will be integrated into the team from day one. You are working on challenging projects related to your studies. Working students at iotec can arrange their employment freely during their studies with flexible working hours and in direct vicinity to the university.
We can offer tasks to prospective engineers and computer scientists as well as students from other areas (e.g. from the field of business administration or natural sciences). Previous knowledge of an area related to our company, for example through internships or training, is desirable.

Application to: info@iotec-gmbh.de
